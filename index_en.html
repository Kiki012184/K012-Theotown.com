Brastokavia is a fictional country belonging to kiki, formerly known as Yakanesia, officially Brastokavia is the Socialist Republic of Brastokavia, Brastokavia has its capital in sadjanore, and is currently led by Nikolav Binareh, and his minister Luna Korskova.
With a GDP of ±1.4 billion dollars and a GDP per capita of ±857 dollars, Brastokavia has its own currency, namely the Brastokan ruble. The economy of Brastokavia is mostly driven by the agricultural, forestry, recycling and production of heavy goods.
Brastokavia has 9.7 million people, the population is small but very diverse, with a population density of 552.05 people/KM^2, the brastokan ethnicity is the majority of all residents, with a percentage of 92%, the rest are brastokavian citizens with ethnic neighbors, namely Kartoskavan 4%, and Lunarayan 2%, the rest are from other ethnicities, including ethnicities from other countries.
Not only that, the country of Brastokavia also has big cities, such as the city of Nouvecaya, the second largest city in the country, ikrovoni, the old capital of this country, Leukovesna, the cultural city of this country, and finally igralvostok, the most populous port city in the country. .
The official language of Brastokan is Brastokan, Kartoskavan, and Lunasar, with most of the people of Brastokan adhering to Citavana, Islam, and Christianity, and the national animal is the Brastokan fox, and the national anthem is Hey Brastokan!.
