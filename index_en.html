Fictional country

I have several fictional countries to roleplay on several forums and political discord servers, one of which is nation states, most of the fictional countries that I make are socialist countries, because I like real world countries such as North Korea, the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia, and how much the system of socialism is good if you look at the golden age of these countries.

Yakanesia

Yakanesia is a liberal socialist country led by Konovik Sinokaren, with a GDP of 28 billion USD and a population of 9.8 million people, and a moderate government size. Yakanesia is known for its environmental friendliness and the large number of nuclear power plants in this country, and the Yakanesia people enjoy equal economic equality, due to central regulations that free people to do what they like, but no one can be poorer or richer than the people around them, nor can talk about politics. Yakanesia has never participated in any war except for the war of independence which took many lives.

Brastokavia

Brastokavia is a federal liberal state led by Dmitri lynaka, with a GDP of 18.2 Billion USD and a population of 13.5 million, as well as a large government size. Brastokavia is known for its very diverse ethnicity and culture, and the people of Brastokavia are proud of the variety of ethnicities of the country. Art exhibitions are often held in the country's capital, namely Yakurevo, in the midst of the busy city, and various arts from various ethnic Brastokavia are shown. Brastokavia is also known for their advanced water technology, as well as many rail routes through the beautiful area, and efficient rail and ship transportation.

Corksonvile

Corksonvile is an absolute monarchy led by king Thersfon IV, with a GDP of 26.2 billion USD and a population of 6.7 million people, as well as a small government size Corksonvile is known for its busy port of Rockon which is also the capital of this kingdom. However, Corksonvile is also known as a kingdom that often wages war which can explain why this kingdom has a small population but a large GDP, Corksonvile is often easily provoked if someone disagrees with their opinion when there are meetings between countries. Corksonvile is also known for its advanced agricultural technology.

Flugennich

Flugenich is a parliamentary republic led by Klaus Schmidt, with a GDP of 23 Billion USD and a population of 11.8 million people, as well as a moderate size of government, Flugenich is known for their advanced railway technology in the world, beautiful big cities, and the many hydropower plants that make this country is eco-friendly. Apart from that flugenich is known for its highly efficient public transportation which is never late and its very large cycling infrastructure, which makes many people of this country choose to cycle to their work or school,and the government funding and investing in the infrastructure development.
