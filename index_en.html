Fictional Country

I have several fictional countries, which I usually roleplay on several political and country forums or roleplay servers. Most of the fictional countries that I own are fictional countries with a socialist ideology.

Brastokavia

Is a peaceful and prosperous country that is environmentally friendly. Led by Konovik Simokaren, Brastokavia adheres to liberal socialism. With a GDP of 28 billion dollars and a population of 9.8 million, its citizens enjoy a high standard of living. The official language is Brastokan, and the currency is the Brastokavian ruble. Known for its best recycling efforts and extensive nuclear power plants, Brastokavia has remained neutral and has never been involved in any war.

Nokisovgrad

Is a living democratic republic headed by Sobona Kisino. With a GDP of 12 billion dollars and a population of 7.5 million, Nokisovgrad thrives as one of the most democratic countries. The official language is Nokiov, and the currency is the Nokisov Dollar. Currently facing challenges with corrupt politicians, Nokisovgrad offers excellent civil rights. Known for its car-centric culture, Nokisovgrad offers a unique blend of tradition and progress.

Rekovia

Is an absolute monarchy kingdom ruled by King Karl Mexer. With a GDP of $23 billion and a population of 8.8 million, Rekovia exudes power and ambition. The official language is Rekovian, and the currency is the Rokovan Pound. Known for her belligerent tendencies, Rekovia had a reputation for aggressive behavior and being a catalyst for war. Under the reign of King Karl Mexer, Rekovia enacted conscription, raising a strong and formidable army.

Dinofluge

Is a socialist democracy country led by the honorable Great Leader Desfugen Eindofbarn. With a strong GDP of $19 billion and a population of 13.3 million, Dinofluge thrives on its commitment to public welfare. The official language is Flugen, and the currency is Flugen Barls. Known for its outstanding public infrastructure and stunning natural landscapes, Dinofluge embodies environmental friendliness and environmental sustainability. The nation prides itself on its strong emphasis on civic equality, ensuring a harmonious and inclusive society for all its citizens.
