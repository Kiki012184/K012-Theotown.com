I have several fictional countries to roleplay on several forums and political discord servers, one of which is nation states, most of the fictional countries I make are socialist countries, because I like real world countries such as North Korea, the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia, and how much The socialist state system is good if you look at the golden era of these countries.

Socialist Republic of Yakanesia

Yakanesia, is a country with a liberalist ideology of socialism. The capital is Sadjanore and is led by Konovik Sinokaren with Richino Siluyana as prime minister. This country has a mixed theme from several countries at once, namely: Japan, Yugoslavia and Indonesia. It has a population of 9.7 million people with a GDP of 28 billion USD. The country's official language is Yakkan and religiously, Yakanesia has a population that predominantly adheres to Shintoism and Islam, with Orthodox Christians and other religions being a minority.

Federal Republic of Brastokavia

Brastokavia, a country with the ideology of the Federal Republic, has its capital in Solgaragard. Led by Dmitri Lyanka with Lionev Takastan as prime minister, the country is inspired by the culture of the Russian Federation. With a GDP of 18.2 billion USD, Brastokavia's population reaches 13.5 million people. Official languages ​​include Barkos, Kistantan, and Lokatartar, while the majority of the population adheres to Orthodox Christianity and a minority to Islam. Apart from that, Brastokavia is a reform country from Lunaraya which created new countries such as Yakanesia, Hakusei, and others.

Hakusei Empire

Hakusei, an Absolute Monarchy kingdom with its capital in Ryodeku, was led by Emperor Nobishika Naumi with Rachitaku as prime minister. This kingdom is inspired by Japanese culture. With a GDP of 23 billion USD, Hakusei's population reaches 8.8 million people. The country's official language is Hakusai, and the majority of the population adheres to the Shinto religion, and there are few Muslims in the country. This country previously had the name Rekovia.

Republic of Flugenich

Dinofluge, a country with a Socialist Democratic ideology, has its capital in Koslechen and is led by Desfugen Eindofbarn with Sekhtofren Anjute as prime minister. This country has inspiration from German culture. With a GDP of 19 billion USD, Dinofluge's population reaches 13.3 million people. The country's official language is Flugen, and the majority of the population adheres to Orthodox Christianity, Protestant Christianity, and a minority is Muslim.

About Sorane Yoru

Sorane Yoru is my persona in the internet world, similar to a v-tuber. Information about this character is mostly drawn from aspects of my own personality. This character design is deliberately kept simple but attractive.

Design Details

Sorane Yoru has an androgynous appearance with a male identity. His hair is black and his skin is tan. The difference is that his eyes have hetecromia, his right eye is black while his left eye is golden yellow. In everyday wear, Sorane wears a dark green jacket with a beige tank top underneath. His shorts are dark red, and he wears black shoes and white socks. When not wearing a jacket, the appearance looks more relaxed. Apart from that, there is a genderbend variation of this character with longer hair. In this version, Sorane wears a dark red skirt and longer socks.
