Fictional country

I have several fictional countries to roleplay on several forums and political discord servers, one of which is nation states, most of the fictional countries that I make are socialist countries, because I like real world countries such as North Korea, the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia, and how much the system of socialism is good if you look at the golden age of these countries.

Yakanesia

Yakanesia is a socialist republic country which is known as an environmentally friendly country as well as equitable economic equality, currently led by Konovik Simokaren. With a GDP of $28 Billion, and a population of 9.8 Million, its citizens enjoy the public infrastructure provided by the government, they also have many nuclear power plants throughout the country. Yakanesia never waged any war, except for their war of independence.

Brastokavia

Brastokavia is a socialist federal state known as a country with a unique and diverse culture, currently led by Dmitri lynaka. With a GDP of $17 Billion, and a population of 13.2 Million, brastokavia managed to unite several ethnicities at once in one country, after the breakup of the lunaraya federation, also its citizens enjoy highly efficient public transportation. The majority of ethnic groups in Brastokavia are katovian, and several other ethnic minorities.

Corksonvile

Corksonvile is a kingdom known as a kingdom known for one of its busy ports, namely Rockon, currently led by king Thersfon IV. With a GDP of $26.1 Billion, and a population of 6.7 Million, this kingdom is also famous for their highly sophisticated aquatic technology, but this kingdom also often wages wars, and is easily provoked if someone disagrees with their opinion.

Flugennich

Flugennich is a parliamentary republic known as a country that loves public transportation and cycling, currently led by Klaus Schmidt. With a GDP of $23 Billion, and a population of 11.4 Million, its citizens enjoy cycling and public transportation infrastructure that is recognized as the best in the world, because it is never late. Flugennich is also known as an environmentally friendly country.
